About Us

We are your go-to source for high-quality custom spearguns. We believe that every diver should have a speargun that fits them perfectly and performs flawlessly in any underwater environment. That's why we work closely with each customer to design and build a speargun that meets their unique requirements.

Services

What We Do.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced spearo, we have the perfect speargun for you. From small, lightweight guns for shallow-water hunting to powerful, long-range guns for bluewater, we have a wide range of options to choose from.

Custom Spearguns

Our custom spearguns are made from the finest materials and are handcrafted with the utmost care and attention to detail. We use only the highest quality woods, carbon fiber, and stainless steel components to ensure that our spearguns are not only beautiful but also durable and reliable.

Restoration

In addition to our custom speargun design and fabrication services, we also offer restoration and refinishing services for your existing spearguns. Whether your speargun has suffered damage from regular use or has simply lost its luster over time, our team can restore it to its original beauty and functionality. We can replace worn or damaged components, repair dents and scratches, and refinish your speargun to make it look and perform like new again.

CNC Engineering Services

We utilize precision 3 Axis CNC technology to offer custom engraving, inlays and small production projects. Our shop is powered by Carbide 3D's Shapeoko Pro XXL CNC router, which delivers precise, high-quality results that meet the exacting standards of even the most discerning customers.
